Monaco is a city and a tiny state on the Mediterranean coast that borders France. It is known for its chic casinos, the pier where expensive yachts moor, and the stage of the prestigious Formula 1 motor race, which takes place on the streets of Monaco. Monte Carlo, the country's largest district, is home to a magnificent casino complex built during the Belle Epoque and an ornate opera house. There are many luxury hotels, shops, nightclubs and restaurants.
